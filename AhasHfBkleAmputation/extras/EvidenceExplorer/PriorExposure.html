As a sensitivity analysis, each comparison was evaluated in the subpopulations having the following requirements on prior exposure to antihyperglycemic agents (AHAs):

  • No restrictions on prior exposure to AHA.
  • Having at least 1 prior non-metformin AHA exposure in 365 days prior to first exposure to the AHAs in the comparison
  • Having no prior non-metformin AHA exposure in 365 days prior to first exposure to the AHAs in the comparison
  • Having no prior exposure to the comparator drug prior to first exposure to the AHAs in the comparison
