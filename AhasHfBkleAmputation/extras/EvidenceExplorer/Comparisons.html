The following exposure cohorts were compared:
  • new users of canagliflozin
  • new users of empagliflozin
  • new users of dapagliflozin
  • new users of empagliflozin or dapagliflozin (other SGLT2i)
  • new users of any DPP-4 inhibitor, GLP-1 agonist, or other select AHA (select non-SGLT2i)
  • new users of any DPP-4 inhibitor, GLP-1 agonist, TZD, Sulfonylureas, insulin, or other select AHA (all non-SGLT2i)
These specific ingredients are included in the drug classes mentioned above:
  • DPP-4 inhibitors: alogliptin, linagliptin, saxagliptin, sitagliptin, vildagliptin
  • GLP-1 agonists: albiglutide, dulaglutide, exenatide, liraglutide, lixisenatide
  • TZDs: pioglitazone, rosiglitazone, troglitazone
  • Sulfonylureas: glipizide, glyburide, glimepiride, chlorpropamide, tolazamide, tolbutamide, acetohexamide
  • Other select AHAs: acarbose, bromocriptine, miglitol, nateglinide, repaglinide
